Marketing & Communications

Spread the word and build excitement for the conference

Responsibilities:
  • Manage social media campaigns across platforms
  • Create engaging content (graphics, videos, blog posts)
  • Coordinate with sponsors and partners
  • Design promotional materials

Logistics & Operations

Ensure everything runs smoothly on event day

Responsibilities:
  • Coordinate venue setup and breakdown
  • Manage registration and check-in
  • Oversee catering and refreshments
  • Handle day-of troubleshooting

Speaker Relations

Work directly with industry leaders and keynote speakers

Responsibilities:
  • Recruit and coordinate with speakers
  • Manage speaker schedules and logistics
  • Provide speaker support on event day
  • Facilitate Q&A sessions

Technology & Web

Build and maintain our digital infrastructure

Responsibilities:
  • Maintain and update the conference website
  • Manage registration systems
  • Provide technical support during the event
  • Handle AV and streaming setup
