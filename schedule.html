KEYNOTE SPEAKER
FEATURING

Bette Smith

Director, Center for Innovation and Collaboration @ Ball State University

Ms. Smith brings over two decades of experience in higher education, organizational change, and strategic partnership development.

Since 2013, she has served as the Principal of Destra Consulting Group, where she supports corporate, nonprofit, and public-sector clients through transformation initiatives, stakeholder engagement, and leadership alignment.

Previously, she was the Founding Director of the J.P. Morgan Center for Commodities at the University of Colorado Denver Business School. In this role, she established the Center’s academic programs, built strong industry relationships, and led professional events. She also held senior positions at CQG, Inc., a global financial technology firm, where she led corporate communications.

A New York native, Ms. Smith holds a Master’s degree in Social Science from the University of Colorado Denver and a Bachelor’s degree in Russian Studies from Bates College. She is a certified Prosci Change Management Practitioner and serves on several boards focused on leadership and innovation in education.
